## Find and kill 11ty processes

```sh
ps aux | grep eleventy
pkill -f eleventy
```

Btw, same for `tailwind` and `serve`:

```sh
ps aux | grep tailwind
pkill -f tailwind
ps aux | grep .bin/serve
pkill -f .bin/serve
```
