      ## Command Line <small>Tricks</small>

      ### Find and kill <small>11ty processes</small>

      ```sh
      ps aux | grep eleventy
      pkill -f eleventy
      ```

      You can even combine it with other processes hanging around:

      ```sh
      ps aux | grep -E 'eleventy|tailwind|.bin/serve'
      pkill -f tailwind
      pkill -f .bin/serve
      ```
